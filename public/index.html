ThoughtCache allows you to anonymously share your interesting, insightful, or quirky thoughts with others.

  • CACHE allows you to submit a thought.
  • FETCH allows you to see the thoughts everyone has submitted, as well as upvote the thoughts you like and downvote the ones you don't like.

You can post a maximum of one thought per minute, and all thoughts are purged after a week.
